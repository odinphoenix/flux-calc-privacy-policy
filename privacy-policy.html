Information We Collect

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device


  • Calculation History: Saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Never leaves your device.
  • App Settings: Preferences (theme, language, digit grouping) stored locally on your device.
  • Device Storage: The app does not access your photos, media, or external storage. All data is confined to the app's private AsyncStorage.

2. Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)


Firebase may collect and transmit:

  • Screen views and navigation paths
  • Which calculators are used most frequently
  • Session duration and app open frequency
  • Device type, Android OS version, and country/region
  • Search terms entered in the app's search bar
  • App instance ID (randomly generated, non-personal identifier)
  • Firebase Installation ID (randomly generated per-install identifier)
  • Network type (Wi-Fi / mobile data)

This data is anonymised and aggregated. Firebase communicates with: firebaseinstallations.googleapis.com, firebase.googleapis.com, app-measurement.com.


3. Device Identifiers


The app and its third-party SDKs (Firebase, AdMob) may access and transmit the following device identifiers:

  • Android Device ID (ANDROID_ID) — a unique hardware identifier assigned by Android, used by Firebase Analytics
  • Android Advertising ID (AAID / GAID) — a resettable advertising identifier used by Google AdMob
  • Firebase Installation ID — a randomly generated per-install identifier used for analytics

You can reset your Android Advertising ID at any time via Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID.


4. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)


AdMob may collect and transmit:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID) and Android Device ID
  • Device make, model, OS version, and screen resolution
  • IP address (used for regional ad serving; not stored by us)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view time)
  • App version and bundle ID

We request non-personalised ads only. No personal profile is built. AdMob communicates with: googleads.g.doubleclick.net, pagead2.googlesyndication.com, mobileads.google.com, admob.googleapis.com, tpc.googlesyndication.com.


5. Currency Exchange Rate Requests


The currency converter fetches live rates from cdn.jsdelivr.net (@fawazahmed0/currency-api). Only your IP address is incidentally transmitted. No personal data is stored by us or the API provider.


6. Translation Requests


UI strings are translated via DeepL API (api-free.deepl.com) and/or Google Cloud Translation API (translation.googleapis.com). Only UI text strings are sent — never your calculations or personal data.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Firebase Analytics to improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): AdMob advertising data based on consent to non-personalised ads. Withdraw via opt-out steps below.
  • Contractual necessity (Art. 6(1)(b)): Currency and translation requests necessary to provide requested features.

Do Not Sell My Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We do not share your personal data for cross-context behavioural advertising or targeted advertising.


Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and similar laws, you have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. Because we do not sell personal information, no opt-out of sale is required. However, you can limit data collection at any time:


To opt out of targeted advertising (AdMob):

  1. Open Android Settings → Google → Ads
  2. Tap "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"
  3. Tap "Reset advertising ID" to clear your Device ID

Note: we already request non-personalised ads by default.


To opt out of analytics (Firebase):

  1. Go to Android Settings → Apps → Flux Calc → Permissions
  2. Revoke data-related permissions

Or email odinphoenix882@gmail.com to request analytics be disabled.


To delete your local data: Open the app → History tab → Clear All, or uninstall the app.


To submit a Do Not Sell / opt-out request:
Email odinphoenix882@gmail.com with subject "Do Not Sell My Personal Information". We will respond within 15 business days.


Third-party data deletion:

  • Google / Firebase: myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy
  • Google Ads / AdMob: adssettings.google.com
