Information We Collect

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device


  • Calculation History: Results from calculators you use are saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • App Settings: Your preferences (theme, language, digit grouping) are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Storage: The app does not access your photos, media, or external storage. All data is confined to the app's private AsyncStorage.

2. Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)


We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. Firebase may collect and transmit:

  • Screen views and navigation paths
  • Which calculators are used most frequently
  • Session duration and app open frequency
  • Device type, Android OS version, and country/region
  • Search terms entered in the app's search bar
  • App instance ID (a randomly generated, non-personal identifier)
  • Firebase Installation ID (a randomly generated identifier per app install)
  • Network type (Wi-Fi / mobile data)

This data is anonymised and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users. Firebase communicates with: firebaseinstallations.googleapis.com, firebase.googleapis.com, app-measurement.com.


3. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)


We display advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and transmit:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID) — a resettable device identifier
  • Device make, model, OS version, and screen resolution
  • IP address (used to determine region for ad serving; not stored by us)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view time)
  • App version and bundle ID

We request non-personalised ads only. No personal profile is built for targeted advertising. AdMob communicates with: googleads.g.doubleclick.net, pagead2.googlesyndication.com, mobileads.google.com.


4. Currency Exchange Rate Requests


The currency converter fetches live exchange rates from cdn.jsdelivr.net (powered by @fawazahmed0/currency-api). Only your IP address is incidentally transmitted as part of a standard HTTPS request. No personal data is sent or stored by us or the API provider.


5. Translation Requests


If you change the app language, UI strings are translated via DeepL API (api-free.deepl.com) and/or Google Cloud Translation API (translation.googleapis.com). Only the UI text strings (e.g. button labels, section titles) are sent — never your calculations or personal data. No personal identifiers are included in translation requests.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union and European Economic Area, we process personal data under the following legal bases:


  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Firebase Analytics data is processed to improve app performance, fix bugs, and understand usage patterns.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Advertising data via AdMob is processed based on your implicit consent to non-personalised advertising when you use the app. You may withdraw this by opting out as described below.
  • Contractual necessity (Art. 6(1)(b)): Currency exchange rates and translation requests are necessary to provide the features you explicitly request.

Advertising

FLUX CALC displays interstitial advertisements powered by Google AdMob. Ads are shown after you perform calculations, capped at a maximum of 5 ads per day.


We request non-personalised ads only (requestNonPersonalizedAdsOnly = true). Ads are served based on context — not based on your personal profile, browsing history, or behaviour across other apps.


For more information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

How to Opt Out of Advertising & Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data and do not use it for targeted advertising. However, if you wish to further limit data collection:


Opt out of AdMob advertising:

  1. Open Android Settings
  2. Go to Google → Ads
  3. Tap "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" and/or "Reset advertising ID"

Since we already request non-personalised ads, this is our default behaviour.


Opt out of Firebase Analytics:

  1. Go to Android Settings → Apps → Flux Calc → Permissions
  2. Revoke any data collection permissions

Or email odinphoenix882@gmail.com to request analytics be disabled for your installation.


Delete your local data:

Open the app → History tab → Clear All, or simply uninstall the app.


Request deletion of third-party data:

  • Firebase / Google: myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy
  • AdMob / Google Ads: adssettings.google.com

For all other opt-out requests, email odinphoenix882@gmail.com.

Data Retention

  • Firebase Analytics data is retained by Google for up to 14 months, then automatically deleted.
