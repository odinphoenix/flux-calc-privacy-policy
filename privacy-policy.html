Privacy Policy

// Last Updated: May 2026

01 — Introduction

Welcome to FLUX CALC. Your privacy is a priority. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you use our professional calculator suite.

By using FLUX CALC, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

02 — Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

💾
Calculation History Results from calculators you use are saved locally on your device via AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
⚙️
App Settings Your preferences (theme, language, digit grouping) are stored locally on your device only.

Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)

📊
Usage Analytics We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This collects: screen views and navigation paths, which calculators are used most, session duration and app open frequency, device type, OS version and country/region, and search terms entered in the app's search bar. This data is anonymised and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users.

Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

📢
Non-Personalised Ads We display advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect: Advertising ID (Android Ad ID), device information (model, OS version), and IP address for regional ad serving. We request only non-personalised ads — no personal profile is built for targeted advertising.

Network Requests

🌐
Currency Exchange Rates The currency converter fetches live rates from a public CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net). Only your IP address is incidentally transmitted as part of a standard HTTPS request. No personal data is sent.
🌍
Translation Requests If you change the app language, UI strings are translated via DeepL and/or Google Translate APIs. Only UI text strings (not your calculations) are sent. No personal identifiers are included.
03 — How We Use Your Information
🔍
App Improvement Analytics data is used solely to improve the app — fixing bugs, prioritising features, and understanding usage patterns.
📣
Advertising Ad data is used by Google AdMob to serve relevant, non-personalised advertisements.
🚫
No Data Sales We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
04 — Advertising

FLUX CALC displays interstitial advertisements powered by Google AdMob. Ads are shown after you perform calculations, with a maximum of 5 ads per day.

We request non-personalised ads only (requestNonPersonalizedAdsOnly = true), meaning ads are not targeted based on your personal profile or browsing history.

Google's Ad Policy: For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, visit policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
05 — Third-Party Services

FLUX CALC uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

07 — Data Retention
📱
Local Data History and settings remain on your device until you clear them via the app's History screen or uninstall the app.
📊
Analytics Data Retained by Google Firebase for up to 14 months per Google's standard retention policy.
📢
Ad Data Managed by Google AdMob per their data retention policies.
08 — Your Rights
📋
Access Request a copy of data we hold about you.
🗑️
Deletion Delete your locally stored data using the "Clear History" option in the app, or by uninstalling.
🚫
Opt Out of Ad Personalisation Go to Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
📤
Data Portability Your local calculation history can be cleared at any time from the History tab.
09 — Data Security

All local data is stored using AsyncStorage on your device. Network requests to third-party APIs use HTTPS encryption. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.
