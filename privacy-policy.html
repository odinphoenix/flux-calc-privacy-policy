Privacy Policy

// Last Updated: April 2026

01 — Introduction

Welcome to FLUX CALC. Your privacy is a priority. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our industrial and professional calculator suite.

02 — Data Collection

FLUX CALC is designed to function primarily as a local tool. Here's what we collect:


💾
Local Storage Your calculation history and app settings are stored locally on your device.
🔐
Authentication If you sign in using Google or Email, we store basic profile information (name, email) via Firebase to sync your data across devices.
📊
Usage Data We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve tool accuracy.
03 — Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for specific features:


Firebase — Secure authentication and data synchronization
OpenER-API — Real-time currency exchange rates
Exchangerate-API — Real-time currency exchange rates


No personal identifiers are sent during currency rate requests.

04 — Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, as certain data is stored locally, the security of your device remains your responsibility.

05 — Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the official support channels provided in the app on Google Play Store.

This privacy policy applies to the Flux Calc Android application.
