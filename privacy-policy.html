Information We Collect

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device


  • Calculation History: Results from calculators you use are saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • App Settings: Your preferences (theme, language, digit grouping) are stored locally on your device.

2. Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)


We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This may collect:

  • Screen views and navigation paths
  • Which calculators are used most frequently
  • Session duration and app open frequency
  • Device type, operating system version, and country/region
  • Search terms entered in the app's search bar
  • App instance ID (a non-personal random identifier)

This data is anonymised and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users from this data.


3. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)


We display advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address (used for regional ad serving only)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

We request non-personalised ads only. No personal profile is built for targeted advertising.


4. Currency Exchange Rate Requests


The currency converter fetches live rates from a public API (cdn.jsdelivr.net / @fawazahmed0). Only your IP address is incidentally transmitted as part of a standard HTTPS request. No personal data is sent or stored by us.


5. Translation Requests


If you change the app language, UI strings are translated via DeepL API and/or Google Translate API. Only the UI text strings (not your calculations or personal data) are sent. No personal identifiers are included in these requests.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union and European Economic Area, we process personal data under the following legal bases:


  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Firebase Analytics data is processed to improve app performance, fix bugs, and understand usage patterns.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Advertising data via AdMob is processed based on your implicit consent to non-personalised advertising when you use the app. You may withdraw this by opting out as described in the Your Rights section.
  • Contractual necessity (Art. 6(1)(b)): Currency exchange rates and translation requests are necessary to provide the features you explicitly request.
How We Use Your Information
  • Analytics data is used solely to improve the app — fixing bugs, prioritising features, and understanding usage patterns.
  • Ad data is used by Google AdMob to serve non-personalised advertisements.
  • Currency and translation data is used to provide the features you request in real time.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

Advertising

FLUX CALC displays interstitial advertisements powered by Google AdMob. Ads are shown after you perform calculations, capped at a maximum of 5 ads per day.


We request non-personalised ads only (requestNonPersonalizedAdsOnly = true). Ads are served based on context — not based on your personal profile, browsing history, or behaviour across other apps.


For more information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Data Retention
  • Local data (history, settings) remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the app. We have no access to this data.
  • Firebase Analytics data is retained by Google for up to 14 months, then automatically deleted.
  • AdMob advertising data is retained by Google per their policies (typically up to 90 days for ad interaction data).
  • Currency and translation requests are transient — no data is retained by us after the request completes.
